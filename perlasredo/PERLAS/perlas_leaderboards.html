About the Leaderboards

The Perlas Leaderboards showcase the top fishers in the community. Rankings are based on the total number of fish caught, achievements unlocked, and the rarity of fish collected.

  • Rank: Position in the leaderboard
  • Fish Caught: Total fish collected
  • Achievements: Special milestones unlocked
  • Common/Rare/Legendary: Types of fish caught

Keep fishing and learning to climb the ranks!
